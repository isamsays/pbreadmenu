Best Picks from the Panera Bread Breakfast Menu

1. Signature Breakfast Sandwiches

Panera's breakfast sandwiches are a top choice for their flavor, freshness, and satisfying portions. Some standouts include:

  • Bacon, Egg & Cheese on Brioche: Fluffy eggs, crispy bacon, and cheese on a buttery brioche bun.

  • Sausage, Egg & Cheese on Asiago Bagel: Savory sausage and melty cheese paired with the unique flavor of an Asiago bagel.

  • Avocado, Egg White, Spinach, and Cheese on Multigrain Bagel Flat: A lighter, vegetarian option packed with flavor and nutrients.

Why we love them:These sandwiches are filling, customizable, and made with cage-free eggs. The multigrain and ciabatta options add a hearty, artisanal touch.

2. Baked Egg Soufflés

For a more decadent breakfast, Panera's soufflés are a must-try:

  • Four Cheese Soufflé: A blend of cheeses in a flaky pastry shell.

  • Spinach & Artichoke Soufflé: Creamy, savory, and perfect for veggie lovers.

  • Spinach & Bacon Soufflé: The perfect balance of smoky bacon and fresh spinach.

Why we love them:Soufflés are light, fluffy, and rich, making them a satisfying treat for special mornings.

3. Wholesome Bowls and Oatmeal

If you prefer a lighter, yet filling start:

  • Steel-Cut Oatmeal with Strawberries & Pecans: Packed with fiber and topped with fresh fruit and nuts.

  • Greek Yogurt with Mixed Berries Parfait: Creamy yogurt, crunchy granola, and sweet berries for a balanced breakfast.

Why we love them:These options are loaded with nutrients and keep you energized without feeling heavy.

4. Fresh Baked Goods and Pastries

Panera's bakery is legendary. For a quick bite or a sweet treat:

  • Muffins, Scones, and Muffies: Rotating flavors, always fresh.

  • Bear Claw and Cinnamon Roll: Perfectly sweet, ideal for pairing with coffee.

5. Breakfast Bundles for Families

  • Breakfast Sandwich Feast: Four sandwiches of your choice-great for sharing.

  • Fresh Baked Goods Feast: Six pastries, including muffins and cinnamon rolls.

6. Refreshing Beverages

  • Coffee and Espresso Drinks: Wide variety, including lattes and cold brews.

  • Smoothies: Strawberry Banana, Mango, and more-made with real fruit.

  • Charged Lemonades: For a flavorful caffeine boost.
